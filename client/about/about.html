History
Madhyam was launched on 16 November 2016.Our CEO Mr. Raja Ji Pandey had a dream to see the poor children educated, children who can't study for the sake of money they should be studied. That's why our CEO Raja Ji Pandey launched this student organisation for educating the poor and needy children and to help the poor people.This was just a thought that is being smoothly executed. And now we are providing education to some children of an orphanage, and slowly we are growing and growing. We even help poor people by donating them clothes, foods, etc. This is just not a dream of our CEO but also a dream of every other person involved in this Madhyam. Our moto is just to make the children that capable that they can face any situation in this world.Hope we reach to success and make the world better.
