History

Madhyam was launched on 16 November 2016. Our CEO Mr. Raja Ji Pandey had a dream that the poor children must be educated, they are not able to study because they need to earn bread and butter for themselves.

That's why our CEO Raja Ji Pandey launched this NGO for educating the poor and needy children. This was just a thought that is being smoothly executed. And now we are providing education to children from orphanages and we are growing at a steady pace. We also help the poor people by donating them clothes, food, etc.

This is just not a dream of our CEO but also a dream of every other person involved in this good cause. Our moto is just to make the children capable of doing anything in their lives so that they can face any situation in this world. Hope we succeed in our cause and make this world a better place to live.
