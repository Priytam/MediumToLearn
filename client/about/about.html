About

Madhyam was launched on 16 November 2016. Our CEO Mr. Raja Ji Pandey dreams that in the future all poor children will be educated. Many of them are deprived from going to school. He aims that Madhyam would help them achieve a better life and earn their own bread and butter.

That is why we launched this NGO for the benefit of rural children . This began as a thought that is now being smoothly executed. Thus, we are supporting destitute children from orphanages and rural areas. We are growing at a steady pace. We also donate basic requirements like clothes and food to sustain them.

This is not just a dream of our CEO but also a dream of every other person involved in this good cause. Our motto is to make the children capable of standing on their own feet so that they become a part of this country's development. Hope we succeed in our cause and make this world a better place to live for the children .
