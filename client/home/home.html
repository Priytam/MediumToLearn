Welcome to Maadhyam.

Madhyam means path, and we are creating a path for the poor children to success.

We provide digital education and basic communication skills to the poor and needy children.

As we know in this growing world nobody can survive without computers and communication so we are helping the poor and needy people to be capable of surviving in this world of technology.
